# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

CS students, founders, recruiters, and collaborators visiting Sanath's personal portfolio. They want to quickly understand who he is, what he builds (Vardio), and whether he's worth talking to.

## Product Purpose

A personal portfolio that establishes Sanath as a serious builder and founder. Conveys technical taste, product instinct, and founder energy through design and content.

## Brand Personality

Builder, technical, clean, confident. No fluff. Words earn their place.

## Anti-references

Dark "hacker" terminal themes, generic SaaS landing pages, over-designed portfolios that prioritize flash over substance, glassmorphism, side-stripe borders, gradient text.

## Design Principles

- Substance over style — every element serves a purpose
- Fast by default — zero unnecessary weight
- Personal without being informal — confident, not casual
- Design that disappears — the work speaks, not the chrome

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA compliance target. Light theme must be comfortable for extended reading.
